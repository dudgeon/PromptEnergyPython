Prompt⚡️Energy is a personal project to visualize some I deas I have in my head about how large, regulated entities can safely arm their associates with LLM super-powers in ways that are consistent with their risk standards, processes, and cultures.

While open-ended, conversational use of tools like ChatGPT are clearly the future (ChatGPT itself was instrumental in writing this demo app), I am interested in finding ways to accomodate the legitimate concerns of organizations that may struggle to govern and adopt these tools.
